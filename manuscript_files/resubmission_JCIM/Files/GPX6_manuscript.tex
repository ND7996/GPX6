\section*{Data and Software Availability}

The manuscript source files, figure material, and analysis files are available from the GPX6 GitHub repository (\url{https://github.com/ND7996/GPX6}). The molecular simulation dataset generated in this study is available through the Catalan Open Research Area (CORA) Research Data Repository managed by the Consortium of University Services of Catalonia (CSUC; \url{https://doi.org/10.34810/DATA3330}). The manuscript and Supporting Information identify the molecular simulation, EVB, alignment, and sequence-design software used for the calculations.
